Privacy Policy

BabyPulse Pro Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how BabyPulse Pro handles information when you use the app.

Information We Process

BabyPulse Pro allows you to record baby care information such as sleep sessions, feeding logs, diaper changes, reminder preferences, and premium entitlement status needed for app functionality.

How Information Is Used

The information you enter is used to provide the app’s core features, including activity tracking, timeline views, statistics, reminders, and premium feature access.

Data Storage

BabyPulse Pro stores app data locally on your device. The app does not require account creation to use its core features.

Notifications

If you grant permission, BabyPulse Pro can send local notifications on your device for feed reminders and sleep check reminders.

Subscriptions

Premium subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive your full payment information. Subscription management, billing, and cancellations are handled by Apple.

Tracking and Advertising

BabyPulse Pro does not use your information for third-party advertising or cross-app tracking.

Data Sharing

BabyPulse Pro does not sell your personal information. Shared Tracker is a user-initiated one-time export and import flow between devices.

Children's Privacy

BabyPulse Pro is intended for use by parents and caregivers as a baby care tracking tool. It is not directed to children to use on their own.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes means the updated policy will apply.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact support@babypulsepro.app.
