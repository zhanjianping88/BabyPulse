Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I restore my subscription? Open the premium screen in the app and tap Restore Purchases.
  • Does BabyPulse Pro require an account? No. The app does not require sign-in.
  • How do reminders work? BabyPulse Pro uses local notifications on your device for feed reminders and sleep check reminders.
  • How does Shared Tracker work? Shared Tracker creates a one-time code that can be imported on another device. It is not continuous cloud sync.
  • Where is my data stored? BabyPulse Pro stores tracking data on your device.

Subscriptions

Subscriptions are billed through Apple. You can manage or cancel your subscription in your Apple ID account settings.
